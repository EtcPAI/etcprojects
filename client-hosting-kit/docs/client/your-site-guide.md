## What's safe to change yourself (and what isn't)

You don't have direct edit access — and that's intentional. It means:

✅ **You can't accidentally break the site.** Every change goes through us.
✅ **Every change is backed up.** We can roll back instantly if needed.
✅ **Your content is safe** — nothing here lives only on your laptop.

If you ever want direct edit access for parts of the site (e.g. a blog), let
us know. We can wire up a simple editor for those sections.
