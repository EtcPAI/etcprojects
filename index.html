Our Story

Built from the
inside out.

We started Etc. Projects to solve a problem we kept seeing. Independent financial advisors trying to run modern, ambitious businesses and doing it brilliantly, but often without the right tools, the right support, or a partner who understood the whole picture.

You deserve better.

We knew we could build it, so we rolled up our sleeves and went to work. Five hundred transformations later, we have a system and proven method that is rooted in advisor reality. A process we have used, refined, and used again. Successfully.

We are tech agnostic but never neutral. We test, use, and prove every solution we recommend. If we cannot stand behind it, we do not put it in front of you.

Etc. AI Program

AI that knows
your practice.

Any team that has been through our practice transformation program knows just how committed we are to supporting your practice by mitigating risk. We are unwavering in our efforts and help you build upon a solid foundation with clear guardrails. AI adoption included.

Most AI tools are built without context. They do not know your obligations, your products, your compliance posture, your team, or how you talk to clients. You may not know the boundaries you need to work within. The Etc. AI Program configures AI to work the way your practice works, gives you ready-to-use workflows for the things you do every day, and trains you in context every step of the way with risk and compliance at the heart of our program. This is what ethical stewardship and independence look like in 2026.
