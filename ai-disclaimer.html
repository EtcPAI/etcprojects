AI Use Disclaimer

We use AI-assisted tools to support transcription, analysis, and strategic work. Here is what that means and what we ask of you.

In Effect · Reviewed Regularly

Disclaimer

49Maple Inc. o/a Etcetera Projects ("Etcetera Projects") prepares summaries, notes, analyses, and strategic recommendations using a combination of human expertise and AI-assisted tools. These tools may include transcription software, pattern analysis, and content-distillation systems designed to increase efficiency and accuracy.

While we take great care in our review and quality control processes, AI-generated transcripts and summaries may contain errors, omissions, or misinterpretations. This can happen for several reasons, including audio quality, linguistic nuance, contextual complexity, or system limitations.

Your Review Matters

Because our work relies heavily on the information you provide, it is essential that you review all documents, summaries, and strategic materials we prepare for accuracy and completeness. Please notify us promptly if:

  • Any detail is incorrect or incomplete
  • You believe something is missing or misunderstood
  • Your instructions, preferences, or circumstances change

Your review ensures the integrity of your records and supports the quality, suitability, and reliability of any recommendations or plans we develop together.

What You Acknowledge

By engaging with Etcetera Projects, you acknowledge and accept that:

  1. AI-assisted tools may be used to support transcription, analysis, and strategic formulation
  2. Human error or AI error may occur despite reasonable care
  3. You are responsible for reviewing all materials provided to you and identifying any corrections needed

This collaborative verification process protects your business, strengthens the accuracy of your operational and compliance frameworks, and ensures the highest standard of service delivery.
